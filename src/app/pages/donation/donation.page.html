Why Donate?

At My Crohn's and Colitis Angel (MyCCAngel), we're dedicated to supporting individuals with Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD). Our mission is to provide a nurturing social platform for IBD patients to find community, resources, and encouragement.

Your donation helps us:

  • Maintain a supportive online community
  • Provide educational resources
  • Offer expert advice and support groups
  • Improve our online community platform
  • Provide financial assistance for medications and doctor visits
  • Host future events for IBD patients
